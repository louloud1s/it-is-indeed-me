(L)OVEREMOTIONAL DIARY


Created in the midst of the coronavirus pandemic, this video collage is an attempt to document feelings and new realities rising in the situation of mandatory isolation. Heavy use of instagram filters, virtual erotic reality and world simulations create a new landscape where an individual’s existence is forcibly unattached from their physicality. However, even if the forming of this new sort of identity may seem freeing, it is still molded under the infulence of the same commercializing values that turn even a communist anthem into a franchise with cute girls.
